ABOUT US

Get to know Sixteen Clothing

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Sixteen Clothing began as a small boutique in New York City with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, fashionable clothing at affordable prices. What started as a single store has now grown into a beloved brand with customers across the country.

Our name "Sixteen" represents the sweet sixteen - a time of transformation, growth, and finding one's personal style. We aim to help our customers express their unique identities through our clothing collections.

Our Mission

At Sixteen Clothing, we believe that everyone deserves to look and feel their best without breaking the bank. We're committed to:

  • Providing high-quality fashion at accessible prices
  • Promoting sustainable and ethical manufacturing practices
  • Celebrating diversity and inclusivity in all our designs
  • Delivering exceptional customer service

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through sustainable practices.

Ethical Production

All our partners follow ethical labor practices and provide fair working conditions.

Quality

We never compromise on quality, ensuring our products stand the test of time.

Customer Satisfaction

Your happiness is our priority, with easy returns and responsive support.
